ICP Framework

Who we target

A precise ideal customer profile drives every sourcing, enrichment, and scoring decision.

Target Persona

  • CTO / VP Engineering / Head of AI-ML
  • Series B-D, 200-5,000 employees
  • LLMs running in production
  • AI/ML infra spend signals
  • Recent funding (last 18 months)
  • Active ML engineering hiring

Disqualifiers

  • Competitor inference platforms
  • Under 50 employees
  • Government / defense
  • No self-hosted infra
  • Educational institutions
  • Product-only companies

A/B Testing

Controlled variants for top 20

Top 20 leads (score 88+) get two variants. 50/50 randomized, 2-week window, 90% significance target.

Variant A — Pain-Led

Open with the exact technical pain

"Your Ray cluster is probably leaving 30% GPU capacity on the table"
Surfaces the specific GPU efficiency pain tied to their detected stack. Names the exact infra challenge before pitching a solution. Creates immediate relevance for infra-focused CTOs.
Target:12% reply
Open:45%+
Meeting:5%
Variant B — Social Proof

Open with a peer company result

"How a Series C AI company cut inference costs 40% in 3 weeks"
Leads with a concrete, quantified result from a peer company. Curiosity-driven hook reduces resistance. Works for buyers earlier in their evaluation cycle.
Target:10% reply
Open:50%+
Meeting:4%
